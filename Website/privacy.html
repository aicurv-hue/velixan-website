Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

At Velixan Technologies, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your details when you visit our website and request our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you submit a project inquiry or contact us. This includes:

  • Your name and job title
  • Work email address
  • Company name (if provided)
  • Project budget estimates and descriptions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected details to evaluate your project requirements, communicate during clarity calls, and deliver technical services. We do not sell, rent, or lease your contact information to third parties.

3. Data Protection and Confidentiality

Your business concepts and data are protected. Every client engagement begins with a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA). We implement standard security practices to protect your communications and prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics to monitor web traffic and page usage. These analytics tools do not collect personal identifiers or track user actions across third-party websites.

5. Compliance (GDPR & PIPEDA)

We comply with Canadian federal privacy regulations (PIPEDA) and align our data handling operations with international standards, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for visitors from the UK and EU.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or our data management operations, please contact us at hello@velixantechnologies.com.
